Data Deletion Request

Vehicle Management System (VMS) Mobile Application

Your Right to Data Deletion

In accordance with data protection regulations and Google Play Store requirements, you have the right to request the deletion of your personal data from our Vehicle Management System (VMS) mobile application.

Important: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your data is deleted, you will no longer be able to access your account or any associated trip history, fuel records, or other information.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon your request, we will delete the following data associated with your account:

  • Account Information: Username, email, password, and profile details
  • Trip Data: All trip records, routes, and location history
  • Fuel Records: All fuel transaction history
  • Maintenance Logs: Service and maintenance records associated with your trips
  • Uploaded Documents: Any photos or documents you have uploaded
  • Activity Logs: Login history and app usage data

Data That May Be Retained

Note: Some data may be retained for legal, regulatory, or legitimate business purposes:
  • Aggregate/Anonymized Data: Statistical data that cannot identify you
  • Legal Records: Data required for legal compliance or dispute resolution
  • Audit Trails: Records required for organizational compliance and auditing
  • Shared Records: Data that is part of organizational fleet management records

Deletion Process Timeline

1
Request Submission Submit your deletion request using the form below or by contacting us directly.
2
Verification (1-3 business days) We will verify your identity to ensure the request is legitimate.
3
Processing (7-14 business days) Your data will be permanently deleted from our active systems.
4
Confirmation You will receive confirmation once your data has been deleted.
